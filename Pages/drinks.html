1 oz Captain Morgan Parrot Bay coconut rum
1 oz Jagermeister Herbal Liqueur
3 oz pineapple juice
3 oz cranberry juice

Pour 1 oz of Parrot bay into glass over ice, 1 oz of jager, then 3 oz of cranberry juice and pinapple juice. Stir, and serve.
